### WORLD ###

## Where the ship starts off in the universe. Options are: asteroid, space ruins, abandoned fortress, or random
START_LOCATION asteroid

## Enable asteroid tunnel/cave generation. Will behave strangely if turned off with a map that expects it on.
#GENERATE_ASTEROID

## Generate loot data.
#GENERATE_LOOT_DATA

## Hash out to disable random events during the round.
ALLOW_RANDOM_EVENTS

### HEALTH ###

## level of health at which a mob goes into continual shock (soft crit)
HEALTH_THRESHOLD_SOFTCRIT 0

## level of health at which a mob becomes unconscious (crit)
HEALTH_THRESHOLD_CRIT -50

## level of health at which a mob becomes dead
HEALTH_THRESHOLD_DEAD -100

## Determines whether limbs can be amputated through excessive damage to the organ
## 0 means limbs can't be amputated, 1 means they can
LIMBS_CAN_BREAK 1

### REVIVAL ###

## amount of time (in hundredths of seconds) for which a brain retains the "spark of life" after the person's death (set to -1 for infinite)
REVIVAL_BRAIN_LIFE -1

### Miscellaneous ###

## Config options which, of course, don't fit into previous categories.

## If security is prohibited from being most antagonists
#PROTECT_ROLES_FROM_ANTAGONIST

## Reduces view range when wearing welding helmets
WELDER_VISION

## Enter the hexcode for the light given off by space/stars
STARLIGHT #FFFFFF

## Allows shooting between z-levels
Z_LEVEL_SHOOTING
